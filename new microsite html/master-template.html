📋 HOW TO USE THIS TEMPLATE

Quick Start:

  1. Change the title in the <title> tag
  2. Delete this yellow instruction box
  3. Choose your hero style (simple or with video)
  4. Pick your sections and components from below
  5. Update the content with your information
  6. Delete unused component examples
  7. Upload to Google Sites

Available Sections:

  • Hero: Green, Purple, or Orange gradient
  • Hero with Video: For product pages
  • Sticky Navigation: For pages with multiple sections
  • Section Backgrounds: White, Gray, or Black
  • Content Grids: 2, 3, or 4 columns

Component Library Included:

  • ✅ Training Cards (green hover)
  • ✅ Competitor Cards (green hover)
  • ✅ Demo Resource Cards (purple hover)
  • ✅ Playbook Cards (purple theme)
  • ✅ Product Feature Cards (green hover)
  • ✅ Standard Content Cards (purple hover)
  • ✅ All button styles
  • ✅ CTA sections
  • ✅ Filter sections

⚠️ The anchor fix script is already included at the bottom - don't remove it!
