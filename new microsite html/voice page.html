What it is

Sidekick Voice brings generative AI phone support – humanlike support via the phone channel. Unlike inflexible IVRs, conversational IVRs, or voice menus, Sidekick can have real-time, human-like conversations with customers calling in.

Real Time Resolutions

Handles inquiries in real-time with natural conversation

Elevates User Experience

No more rigid IVR menus or frustrating phone trees

Smart Escalation

Seamlessly transfers to human agents when needed

Perfect Handoff

Complete conversation context transfers to agents

Available 24/7

Round-the-clock phone support without staffing costs

Maintains Brand Quality

Consistent, on-brand conversations every time
