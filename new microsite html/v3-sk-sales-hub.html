What is Sidekick Sales?

Sidekick Sales is AI designed specifically for revenue generation. It engages website visitors, qualifies leads, books meetings, and nurtures prospects through the sales funnel.

Unlike traditional chatbots that deflect, Sidekick Sales actively converts visitors into qualified opportunities and drives measurable revenue impact.
