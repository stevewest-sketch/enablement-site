What it is

Sidekick Sales is an AI-powered shopping assistant that transforms browsers into buyers. It leverages Gladly's customer profiles and commerce integrations to suggest credible product answers, and recommendations in real time—helping shoppers buy with confidence and turning support into sales.

Why it matters: Online shopping often lacks personal touch, causing hesitation, cart abandonment, and missed upsell opportunities. Sidekick Sales removes the friction by answering pre-purchase questions instantly, recommending products with full context, increasing conversion rates, and proving the revenue impact of customer service.

Who it's for: B2C Businesses and eCommerce businesses with significant online traffic and revenue goals—especially those with pressure to grow revenue without increasing ad spend.
