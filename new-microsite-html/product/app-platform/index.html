What it is

Gladly's App Platform is a low-code integration layer that connects external systems like order management, loyalty, and payment directly to Gladly's interface for agents and AI.

Why it matters: It eliminates tool-switching, gives agents full contact, and lets AI act on real data‚Äîteams get faster setup, better service outcomes, and 'lower IT overhead.

Who it's for: For IT and CX teams at ecommerce high-tech customers who rely on multiple systems to support customers and need simple tools to unify data across agents and AI.
